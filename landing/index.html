How do I use this?

Click "Download MilCalc" above. Save the HTML file. Double-click it.

The app opens in your browser and runs entirely on your computer. Plug in your numbers, see your full transition financial picture.

That's it. No account. No signup. No internet required.

For TAP offices, Fleet and Family Support, financial counselors: this same file works for anyone you point to it. Share the link, point service members at it. No setup on your end.

I built this because transitioning service members deserve to see their full financial picture before signing their DD-214, without paying someone to show it to them. Now anyone can download it and have answers in 30 seconds.

If your organization wants to host a copy or include this in your transition program, the GitHub repo has everything you need. MIT licensed. Take it.
