Forecast your military transition, down to the dollar.

MilCalc is a free military financial calculator. Plug in your numbers and see your full transition picture — retirement pay, TSP, GI Bill, VA compensation, VGLI, state taxes, and cost-of-living comparisons across locations.

No account. No tracking. No internet required. You download one file and it runs entirely on your own computer — your numbers never leave your machine.
