# MilCalc Quickstart

1. [Click here to download MilCalc](https://github.com/csimser/milcalc/releases/latest/download/MilCalc.html)
2. Double-click the downloaded `MilCalc.html` file
3. Use it

That's it. The app opens in your browser, runs entirely on your computer, and works offline.

Plug in your numbers and see your transition financial picture. The rate tables (DoD pay tables, BAH rates, VA disability rates, and other financial reference data) are updated annually with each release — download the latest `MilCalc.html` to get current numbers.

If you want to hack on the source code, see [DEVELOPMENT.md](DEVELOPMENT.md).
